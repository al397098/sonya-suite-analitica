V. Strong Sustainability (SS) Assessment

The MRP model enforces a non-compensatory logic. An action is only deemed sustainable if it satisfies the minimum thresholds across all pillars simultaneously.

SS = min(s_Eco, s_Amb, s_Soc)

The verdict is governed by the "weakest link" principle, preventing economic gains from masking environmental degradation.
